A privacy-perserving video doorbell

PrivDoorbell is a open-source video doorbell project. Powered with Onion service, end-to-end encryption and noise features, it provides guarantee on security and privacy. 

Installation Guide Learn more

How it works?

Face detection

Detects face when people come to your door.

Notification

Get notified by the notice, anywhere and at any time which is pushed from the amazing Android app

Management

Manage who can receive the notification.

Open source

The PrivDoorbell project is open-sourced. No backdoors, no advertisements. You can even customize your own doorbell service.

Video Streaming through Tor

The video is served at an onion url from your local device, and streamed through Tor. It ensures that your video is encrypted during transmission and kept safe.

Encryption and noise

PrivDoorbell adapts end-to-end encryption, and add noise to the messages. This prevents anyone including the service provider from accessing your privacy.
